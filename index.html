About Me


Hey there! I'm BetreMariyam Yosef, a 17-year-old teenager hailing from Ethiopia, and I have a fervent passion for photography and graphics design. What's unique about my journey is that I've honed these skills entirely through self-learning. From capturing mesmerizing moments through my camera lens to crafting visually stunning graphics, I'm constantly driven by the desire to express my creativity in innovative ways.

I believe in the power of artistic exploration and pushing the boundaries of what's possible. With a relentless pursuit of excellence, I've evolved from a curious beginner to a dedicated photographer and graphics designer. Every image I capture and every design I create is a testament to my dedication and love for this craft.

And guess what? I've taken my journey a step further by building this very portfolio website from scratch, diving into the world of coding. As a young creator in this ever-evolving digital landscape, I'm excited to showcase my skills and share my passion with the world.

Join me on this exhilarating ride as I capture moments, breathe life into visuals, and embark on a continuous journey of learning and growth.

Let's create art together.
